Goals

This section contains your goals you'll like to follow.
When adding adding a goal you can choose one of the categories for it, you can then later filter your goals per category to get a better overview of a specific domain. Each goal contains habits that you have chosen to add, Accomplishing these habits will improve the score of this goal. Missing a habit or doing a bad habit will change the color of your goal.
The colors are chosen as follow:

  • Green = good job!
  • Orange = needs attention
  • Red = you really should accomplish these habits
A sandglass icon next to a goal means a habit still needs to be accomplished today.

Habits

This section contains the habits linked to the chosen goal.
When you have finished a habit, you can check them of here, this will give you points according to the difficulty assigned to it. Beware, doing a bad habit will lower your score, and not finishing a habit that was due will stop your current streak. So stick to the good habits and do them when they need to be done!
